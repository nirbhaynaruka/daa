  • Internship Fair September 2018

    The 3rd edition of Internship Fair ‘18 was hosted by Directorate of Alumni Affairs. It was a 2-day program where students got the chance to interact with Alumni/other firms and gain a better perspective of what Industry is demanding.

    The firms that offered internship to students include

    • Mr. Umakanthan, Senior Partner & head at Energica (1993 Batch)
    • Mr. Amrit Aacharya, co-founder at Xobin (2015 Batch)
    • Mr. Sugam, Founder, Wisopt (2017 Batch)
    • SRM Innovation and Incubation Center (10+ Companies)
    • IIT Madras Incubation Cell (20+ Companies)
    • Intern Theory (100+ Companies)

  • Internship Fair March 2018

    The second internship fair was conducted with great success in March 2018. More than 300+ students participated in the 2-day event, which took place on the 24th & 25th of March 2018. 10+ companies offered interships to students.

  • Internship Fair March October 2017

    Internship Fair was conducted on 20th and 21st October, 2017. ECS Inc., Global Mantra, Royal Minions, ABB, Philips, Xobin came to offer internships to the students.
